Technical Support:

If you encounter audio or video issues during a call, try restarting the app and checking your internet connection. If the problem persists, contact our support team for further assistance.

Connectivity problems may arise due to network issues or device settings. Ensure that your device has a stable internet connection and that the app has necessary permissions. If issues persist, reach out to our support team for troubleshooting.

While there's no specific internet speed requirement, we recommend a stable connection of at least 1 Mbps for optimal call quality.

Privacy and Security:

Your conversations on the app are encrypted end-to-end, ensuring maximum security and privacy.

You have full control over who can contact you through random calls. You can adjust your privacy settings to specify preferences for incoming calls.

We adhere to strict privacy policies and employ advanced security measures to safeguard your personal information from unauthorized access.

Usage and Features:

The random calling feature connects you with other app users randomly for spontaneous conversations.

You can customize your preferences for random calls, including age range, gender, and interests, to match with like-minded individuals.

In addition to random calling, the app offers features such as messaging, profile customization, and group chats to enhance your communication experience.
